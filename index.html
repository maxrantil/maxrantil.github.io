About Me

Hi, I am a proactive and persistent software developer originally from Sweden, now based in Helsinki, Finland. My journey in the tech industry started at Hive Helsinki coding school, known for its unique peer-learning approach and rigorous curriculum, where I diligently honed my skills in logical thinking and adaptability in facing challenges.

Starting with a foundation in Unix and C programming, I've since rapidly expanded my skillset to include JavaScript and React, applying these skills in my current projects. Additionally, I'm exploring the underlying technologies of Bitcoin through Python, further demonstrating my adaptability to new languages and tools. Moreover, my experiences in peer-to-peer learning have developed my collaborative skills, enabling me to effectively work in diverse teams and contribute to successful projects.

Beyond my technical skills, my constant curiosity and determination fuel my approach. My drive has propelled me into leadership roles at Hive Helsinki, notably when my peers entrusted me to steer a six-member team during an intensive four-month project—The Shell Creation Project (42sh). Our collaborative effort culminated in a robust shell interpreter, a testament to not only my depth of technical expertise but also my capacity as a leader to navigate team dynamics, overcome challenges, and ensure the successful delivery of complex projects.

My dedication to collaboration and making a significant impact is enriched by my experiences as a youth worker and photographer in Sweden, where I developed more patience and understanding of diverse perspectives. This combination of technical skills and interpersonal understanding shapes my approach to software development.
